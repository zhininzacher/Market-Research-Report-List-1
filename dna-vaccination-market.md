<p><p>DNA vaccination is an innovative immunization technique that utilizes genetically engineered DNA to elicit an immune response in the body. This method involves the introduction of plasmid DNA that encodes specific antigens from pathogens, prompting the body to produce its own antibodies and activating T-cells, thus providing immunity against diseases.
