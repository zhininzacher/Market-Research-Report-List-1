<p><p>The RNA Sequencing Technologies market encompasses various types, notably Non-Coding RNA Sequencing and Direct RNA Sequencing. Non-Coding RNA Sequencing focuses on analyzing RNA that does not translate into proteins, playing crucial roles in gene regulation and cellular processes. Direct RNA Sequencing, on the other hand, allows for the real-time analysis of native RNA molecules, preserving their full-length sequences without conversion to cDNA. Together, these technologies provide valuable insights into gene expression and cellular mechanisms, driving advancements in research and personalized medicine.</p></p>
