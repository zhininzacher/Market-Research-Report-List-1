<p><p>The Electrodialysis Reversal (EDR) system market comprises two primary types: Continuous Electrodialysis and Batch Electrodialysis. Continuous Electrodialysis operates in a steady flow, efficiently processing large volumes of wastewater or brine with constant ion removal, ideal for industrial applications. In contrast, Batch Electrodialysis operates in discrete cycles, treating specific volumes of solution, making it suitable for smaller-scale operations or research applications. Both systems utilize ion-selective membranes to separate ions, enhancing water desalination and purification processes.</p></p>
