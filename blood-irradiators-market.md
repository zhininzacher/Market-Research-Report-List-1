<p><strong>The Blood Irradiators Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>X-ray Blood Irradiation</li><li>Gamma-ray Blood Irradiation</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The blood irradiators market comprises two primary types: X-ray blood irradiation and gamma-ray blood irradiation. X-ray blood irradiation utilizes high-energy X-rays to reduce the ability of blood-borne pathogens to proliferate, enhancing the safety of blood transfusions. In contrast, gamma-ray blood irradiation employs gamma radiation, typically from cobalt-60 or cesium-137 sources, effectively sterilizing blood products by disrupting the DNA of pathogens. Both methods aim to improve patient safety in transfusion practices by minimizing the risk of transfusion-transmitted infections.</p></p>
