<p><ul><li>Four Channel, Four Sensor</li><li>Three Channel, Three Sensor</li><li>One Channel, One Sensor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Hydraulic Anti-Lock Braking System (ABS) market is categorized by the number of channels and sensors, each tailored to specific vehicle needs. Four-channel, four-sensor systems independently control braking at each wheel, offering superior performance and safety for high-end vehicles. Three-channel, three-sensor systems manage front wheels separately while linking rear wheels, often used in SUVs. One-channel, one-sensor systems are basic and typically found in smaller vehicles, providing essential anti-lock functionality at a lower cost, making them suitable for budget-conscious consumers.</p></p>
