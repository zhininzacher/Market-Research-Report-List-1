<p><p>The medical ambulatory pumps market comprises several types, including ambulatory feeding pumps, ambulatory infusion pumps, and ambulatory syringe pumps. Ambulatory feeding pumps deliver nutritional fluids to patients outside a hospital setting, ensuring continuous feeding for those unable to eat normally. Ambulatory infusion pumps administer medications or fluids intravenously, providing precise dosing for various therapeutic needs. Ambulatory syringe pumps, designed for portability, deliver small volumes of medication or fluids at controlled rates, enhancing patient mobility and comfort during treatment.</p></p>
