<p><ul><li>Cooled</li><li>Uncooled</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The infrared (IR) cameras market is primarily divided into two types: cooled and uncooled cameras. Cooled IR cameras have a cooling mechanism that reduces the thermal noise, allowing them to detect lower levels of infrared radiation, making them ideal for high-precision applications like military surveillance and research. Uncooled IR cameras, on the other hand, operate at ambient temperatures, are more compact, and are generally less expensive, making them suitable for commercial, automotive, and consumer applications.</p></p>
