<p><p>IVIS Imaging Systems encompass several market types, each catering to specific imaging techniques. Luminescence Imaging Systems utilize light emitted from biological processes, ideal for observing cellular activities. Fluorescence Imaging Systems rely on fluorescent markers to visualize dynamic events within tissues. Isotope Imaging Systems employ radioactive isotopes for tracking biological processes, providing quantitative data. X-Ray Imaging Systems offer high-resolution imaging for anatomical assessment. The "Other" category includes emerging or hybrid technologies that integrate various imaging modalities for enhanced research applications.</p></p>
