<p><p>The Plasma Treatment System market is categorized into high temperature and low temperature segments. High temperature plasma systems operate at elevated thermal levels, effectively modifying surfaces and achieving deep material penetration, ideal for industrial applications like semiconductor fabrication and coatings. In contrast, low temperature plasma systems function at milder thermal conditions, suitable for sensitive materials such as plastics and textiles, ensuring surface activation, cleaning, and modification without damaging substrates. Each type serves specific industry needs based on material properties and processing requirements.</p></p>
