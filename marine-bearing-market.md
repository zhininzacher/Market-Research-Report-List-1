<p><strong>The Marine Bearing Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Rolling Bearing</li><li>Sliding Bearing</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The marine bearing market comprises two primary types: rolling bearings and sliding bearings. Rolling bearings utilize rolling elements like balls or rollers to reduce friction and support loads, offering efficiency and durability in harsh marine environments. In contrast, sliding bearings rely on surfaces sliding against one another, providing simplicity and low maintenance, ideal for applications where ease of installation is crucial. Both types are essential for various marine applications, ensuring reliable performance in ship engines, propellers, and other critical systems.</p></p>
