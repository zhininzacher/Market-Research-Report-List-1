<p><p>The Smart Electrochromic Glass market is categorized into two main types based on the materials used: inorganic and organic electrochromic glass. Inorganic materials typically include metal oxides, offering high stability, durability, and excellent optical performance. They are ideal for architectural applications. On the other hand, organic materials, which include polymers, provide flexibility and can be more cost-effective. However, they may have shorter lifespans and durability issues. Both types are used for energy-efficient solutions in buildings and vehicles.</p></p>
