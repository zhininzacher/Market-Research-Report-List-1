<p><ul><li>"Brushed Motor"</li><li>"Brushless Motor"</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Home Appliances DC Micromotor market is primarily categorized into two types: brushed motors and brushless motors. Brushed motors utilize physical brushes for commutation, allowing for simpler design and lower costs, making them suitable for basic appliances. However, they tend to wear out faster. In contrast, brushless motors employ electronic commutation, resulting in higher efficiency, longer lifespan, and quieter operation, making them ideal for premium appliances like high-performance vacuum cleaners and advanced kitchen devices.</p></p>
