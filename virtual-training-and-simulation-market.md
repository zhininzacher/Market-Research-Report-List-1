<p><p>The Virtual Training and Simulation market encompasses various approaches. In contrast, Virtual Reality-Based Training immerses users in a fully interactive 3D environment using VR headsets, providing a more realistic and engaging experience. Both methods aim to improve learning outcomes and safety in fields like aviation, healthcare, and military, but differ in levels of immersion and technology used.</p></p>
