<p><p>The polycarbonate diol market consists of two main types: solid PCD and liquid PCD. Solid PCD is characterized by its high molecular weight and rigidity, making it suitable for applications requiring durability, such as in coatings and adhesives. In contrast, liquid PCD is more versatile and offers better flow properties, making it ideal for producing elastomers and flexible materials. Both types are crucial in various industries, including automotive, construction, and electronics, due to their excellent thermal and mechanical properties.</p></p>
