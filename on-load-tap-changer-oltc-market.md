<p><p>On-load tap changers (OLTC) are crucial components in power transformers, allowing for voltage regulation while the transformer is energized. The market for OLTCs is primarily categorized into two types: vacuum OLTCs and oil-immersed OLTCs. Vacuum OLTCs operate in a sealed environment, minimizing arcing and maintenance needs, making them suitable for compact applications. Conversely, oil-immersed OLTCs utilize insulating oil for cooling and arc extinction, offering robust performance in larger transformers, particularly in substations and industrial settings.</p></p>
