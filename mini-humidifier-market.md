<p><p>The mini humidifier market features various types, including steam humidifiers and water spray humidifiers. Steam humidifiers work by heating water to produce steam, which is then cooled slightly before being released into the air, providing warm moisture. In contrast, water spray humidifiers emit a fine mist of water droplets into the environment, effectively increasing humidity levels quickly. Both types are popular for personal spaces and portable usage, enhancing comfort and alleviating dryness in the air.</p></p>
