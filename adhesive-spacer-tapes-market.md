<p><p>The adhesive spacer tapes market encompasses two primary types: double-sided and single-sided tapes. Double-sided adhesive tapes feature adhesive on both sides, enabling secure attachment between two surfaces without visible tape, making them ideal for mounting applications. Single-sided adhesive tapes, conversely, have adhesive on only one side, primarily used for bonding or sealing, where one side adheres to a substrate while the other remains exposed. Both types cater to diverse industrial sectors, including electronics, automotive, and construction.</p></p>
