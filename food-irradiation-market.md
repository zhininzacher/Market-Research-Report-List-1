<p><strong>The Food Irradiation Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Electron Beam Radiation</li><li>Gamma Radiation</li><li>X-Ray Radiation</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The food irradiation market consists of three primary types: Electron Beam Radiation, Gamma Radiation, and X-Ray Radiation. Electron Beam Radiation uses high-energy electrons to rapidly treat food, effectively killing pathogens and extending shelf life. Gamma Radiation employs radioactive isotopes, like cobalt-60, for a deeper penetration, making it suitable for bulk food processing. X-Ray Radiation combines aspects of both, utilizing X-rays for effective sterilization with adjustable penetration levels. Each method enhances food safety and longevity while minimizing chemical use.</p></p>
