<p><strong>The Hand Orthoses Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Dynamic Orthotics</li><li>Static Orthotics</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The hand orthoses market is primarily divided into dynamic and static orthotics. Dynamic orthotics are designed to provide support while allowing for movement, aiding in rehabilitation and facilitating functional hand use. These devices often incorporate elastic components or springs to promote mobility. In contrast, static orthotics are primarily used for immobilization, providing stability and protection for injured or post-surgical hands. They are typically rigid and prevent movement, focusing on promoting healing and alignment. Both types play crucial roles in hand therapy and rehabilitation.</p></p>
