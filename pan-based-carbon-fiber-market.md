<p><strong>The PAN-based Carbon Fiber Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Small Tow</li><li>Big Tow</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The PAN-based carbon fiber market can be classified into two main types: small tow and big tow carbon fibers. Small tow fibers typically have fewer filaments per strand, ranging from 1K to 24K, and offer high strength and stiffness, making them ideal for applications in aerospace and high-performance sporting goods. In contrast, big tow fibers, with filament counts of 48K and above, are more cost-effective and are used in larger-scale applications like automotive and industrial components, prioritizing volume and performance efficiency.</p></p>
