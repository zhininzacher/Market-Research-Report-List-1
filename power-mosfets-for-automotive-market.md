<p><p>Power MOSFETs in the automotive market are crucial for managing power efficiently in various applications. N-channel MOSFETs, known for their high efficiency and low on-resistance, dominate due to their excellent performance in switching applications. P-channel MOSFETs, while less common, are used in specific situations where high-side switching is necessary. Dual-channel MOSFETs integrate both N-channel and P-channel technologies, providing versatility and space-saving advantages by enabling bidirectional current flow, crucial for advanced automotive control systems and power management solutions.</p></p>
