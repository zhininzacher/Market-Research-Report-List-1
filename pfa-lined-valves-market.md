<p><p>The PFA lined valves market comprises various types including ball valves, butterfly valves, safety valves, check valves, and gate valves. Ball valves provide reliable sealing for on/off operations, while butterfly valves enable flow regulation with lower pressure drops. Safety valves protect systems from overpressure, and check valves prevent backflow in pipelines. Gate valves are used for full shut-off control. Additionally, other specialized valves cater to unique applications, enhancing performance and durability in corrosive environments, making PFA lining crucial for longevity.</p></p>
