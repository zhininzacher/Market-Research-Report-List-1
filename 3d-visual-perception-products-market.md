<p><strong>The 3D Visual Perception Products Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Structured Light</li><li>iToF</li><li>dToF</li><li>Lidar</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The 3D visual perception products market comprises several key technologies: </p><p>1. **Structured Light**: This method uses projected patterns of light to capture detailed 3D shapes and depths, often utilized in facial recognition and scanning applications. </p><p>2. **iToF (indirect Time of Flight)**: It measures the time light takes to return from the object, providing accurate depth information; commonly used in smartphones and AR devices.</p><p>3. **dToF (direct Time of Flight)**: Similar to iToF but captures light directly, resulting in faster and more precise measurements, ideal for autonomous vehicles.</p><p>4. **LiDAR**: Light Detection and Ranging uses laser pulses to create high-resolution 3D maps, widely used in mapping, forestry, and autonomous navigation.</p><p>5. **Others**: This category includes alternative technologies like stereo vision or hybrid systems, catering to specific industrial or consumer needs.</p></p>
