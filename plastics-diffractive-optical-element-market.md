<p><ul><li>Beam Shaping (Top-Hat)</li><li>Beam Splitting</li><li>Beam Foci</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Plastics Diffractive Optical Element (DOE) market includes various types aimed at manipulating light for specific applications. Beam shaping, particularly in a top-hat profile, redistributes light intensity uniformly across a specified area. Beam splitting involves dividing a light beam into multiple paths with precise control over intensity and angle. Beam focusing refers to converging light into a point or a specific area, enhancing performance in imaging systems. Collectively, these types enhance applications in telecommunications, laser systems, and optical communications.</p></p>
