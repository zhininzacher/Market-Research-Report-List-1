<p><ul><li>Vertical 2D Optical Comparator</li><li>Horizonal 2D Optical Comparator</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The 2D optical comparator market is divided into two main types: vertical and horizontal. Vertical 2D optical comparators utilize a top-down view, allowing operators to examine parts against a projected image from above, ideal for measuring larger and bulkier components. Conversely, horizontal 2D optical comparators present a side view of the workpiece, facilitating the inspection of thinner materials and intricate shapes. Both types serve critical functions in quality control, ensuring precision in manufacturing processes across various industries.</p></p>
