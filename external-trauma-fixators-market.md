These devices consist of pins or screws inserted into the bone, connected to a rigid external frame that maintains alignment during the healing process.</p><p>The External Trauma Fixators Market is poised for substantial growth, expected to rise at a CAGR of 11.3% during the forecast period. Circular fixators offer versatile, adjustable stabilization through concentric rings, ideal for complex injuries.
