<p><ul><li>Non-Porous Cordierite</li><li>Porous Cordierite</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The synthetic cordierite market is divided into two primary types: non-porous cordierite and porous cordierite. Non-porous cordierite is characterized by its dense structure, making it ideal for applications requiring strength and thermal resistance, such as kiln furniture and industrial ceramics. In contrast, porous cordierite features a network of voids, enhancing its ability to facilitate airflow and improve thermal insulation, making it suitable for uses like catalytic converters and porous substrates in various industrial processes.</p></p>
