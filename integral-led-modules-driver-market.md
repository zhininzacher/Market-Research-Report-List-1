<p><p>The Integral LED Modules Driver market consists of two main types: Constant Current Drivers and Constant Voltage Drivers. Constant Current Drivers maintain a consistent current flow to the LED module, ensuring uniform brightness and extending lifespan, ideal for applications requiring stable light output. In contrast, Constant Voltage Drivers provide a fixed voltage, accommodating multiple LEDs in parallel configurations. Each driver type caters to specific lighting requirements, influencing energy efficiency, performance, and compatibility across diverse LED applications.</p></p>
