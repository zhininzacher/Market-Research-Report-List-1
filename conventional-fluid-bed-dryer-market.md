<p><p>The conventional fluid bed dryer market features two primary types: batch operation and continuous operation. In contrast, continuous operation facilitates uninterrupted drying by continuously feeding material through the system, optimizing efficiency and throughput. Both types cater to diverse industries, including pharmaceuticals, food processing, and chemicals, by enhancing drying processes while ensuring consistent product quality.</p></p>
