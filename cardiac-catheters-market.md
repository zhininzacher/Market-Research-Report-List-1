<p><p>The cardiac catheters market comprises various types, each serving distinct diagnostic and therapeutic purposes. Angiography catheters enable imaging of cardiovascular structures; guiding catheters facilitate the placement of other devices. Pulmonary artery catheters monitor heart function and fluid status, while PTCA balloon catheters are used for angioplasty procedures to open narrowed arteries. Intravascular ultrasound (IVUS) catheters provide real-time imaging of arterial walls, and electrophysiology catheters assess and treat heart rhythm disorders, enhancing cardiac care and intervention.</p></p>
