<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Rotary Steerable Drilling System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Push-the-bit Rotary Steerable Drilling System</li><li>Point-the-bit Rotary Steerable Drilling System</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Rotary Steerable Drilling System (RSDS) market consists primarily of two types: Push-the-bit and Point-the-bit systems. Push-the-bit systems use an integrated mechanism to apply lateral force on the drill bit, allowing for precise directional control while drilling. In contrast, Point-the-bit systems incorporate a set of actuated pads that enable directional changes by directly adjusting the bit's angle, offering enhanced maneuverability. Both types enhance drilling efficiency, reduce costs, and improve the safety of drilling operations in complex geological formations.</p></p>
