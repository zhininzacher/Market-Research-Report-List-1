<p><strong>The Nanoindentation Tester Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Conventional Nanoindentation Tester</li><li>In-Situ Nanoindentation Tester</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The nanoindentation tester market comprises two main types: conventional and in-situ nanoindentation testers. Conventional nanoindentation testers are designed for standard testing of material hardness and elastic properties under controlled conditions, making them ideal for laboratory applications. In contrast, in-situ nanoindentation testers enable real-time characterization of materials during various processes, such as heating or mechanical loading, allowing for a deeper understanding of material behavior under operational conditions. Both types serve critical roles in materials science and engineering research.</p></p>
