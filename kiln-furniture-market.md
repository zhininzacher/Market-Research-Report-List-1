<p><p>The kiln furniture market encompasses various materials used to support and stabilize ceramics during firing. Silicon carbide offers high thermal conductivity and strength, making it suitable for extreme temperatures. Cordierite, known for its thermal shock resistance, is ideal for high-fired ceramics. Mullite provides excellent thermal stability and mechanical properties, while alumina is valued for its durability and resistance to wear. Each material serves specific applications, catering to different firing conditions and product requirements in the ceramics industry.</p></p>
