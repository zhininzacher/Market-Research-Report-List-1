<p><p>The coating pigments market is divided into two main types: organic and inorganic pigments. Organic coating pigments are derived from carbon compounds, offering vibrant colors and excellent transparency, commonly used in high-quality applications like automotive and decorative paints. In contrast, inorganic coating pigments, made from minerals and metal oxides, provide superior opacity, durability, and resistance to environmental factors. They are typically found in industrial coatings and construction materials, where longevity and performance are paramount.</p></p>
