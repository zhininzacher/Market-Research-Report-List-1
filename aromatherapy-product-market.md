<p><ul><li>Topical Application</li><li>Aerial Diffusion</li><li>Direct Inhalation</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The aromatherapy product market includes three main types: topical application, aerial diffusion, and direct inhalation. Topical application involves applying essential oils directly to the skin for therapeutic benefits, often mixed with carrier oils. Aerial diffusion disperses essential oils into the air using diffusers, creating a fragrant environment that may promote relaxation or mood enhancement. Direct inhalation entails inhaling essential oils from the bottle or a pad, providing immediate effects on respiratory and emotional well-being. Each method caters to varying consumer preferences and wellness goals.</p></p>
