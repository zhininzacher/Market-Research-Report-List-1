<p><p>Ventilation boxes are essential components in HVAC systems, categorized into active and passive markets. Active ventilation boxes use fans or blowers to control air movement, allowing for precise ventilation rates and improved indoor air quality. In contrast, passive ventilation boxes rely on natural airflow, utilizing pressure differences and temperature variations to facilitate ventilation without mechanical assistance. Both types play crucial roles in maintaining optimal indoor environments, addressing energy efficiency, and ensuring compliance with health and safety standards.</p></p>
