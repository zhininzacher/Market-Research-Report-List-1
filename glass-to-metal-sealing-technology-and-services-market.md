Two primary market types include glass-metal matching sealing, where specific materials are precisely matched for thermal expansion to minimize stress and enhance longevity, and glass-metal compression sealing, which utilizes pressure to achieve a tight fit between the glass and metal. Both methods are essential in industries like electronics, aerospace, and medical devices, enhancing overall product reliability.</p></p>
