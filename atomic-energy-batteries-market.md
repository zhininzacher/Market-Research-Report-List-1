<p><strong>The Atomic Energy Batteries Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Thermal Conversion Type</li><li>No-Thermal Conversion Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The atomic energy batteries market is categorized into two main types: thermal conversion and non-thermal conversion. </p><p>Thermal conversion batteries utilize heat generated from radioactive decay to produce electrical energy. This method often involves thermoelectric materials that convert temperature differences into electricity.</p><p>In contrast, non-thermal conversion batteries harness the direct energy released from radioactive processes, such as alpha or beta decay, without relying on heat. This direct energy conversion typically results in higher efficiency and longer operational lifespans, making both types valuable for specific applications.</p></p>
