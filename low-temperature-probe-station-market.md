<p><p>The low temperature probe station market features three primary types: automatic, semi-automatic, and manual probe stations. Semi-automatic probe stations offer a balance, requiring some manual intervention while still providing automation benefits, making them versatile for various research needs. Manual probe stations, on the other hand, rely exclusively on user operation, providing flexibility and control, often used for specialized or detailed experimental setups.</p></p>
