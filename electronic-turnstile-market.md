<p><p>The electronic turnstile market is characterized by three main types: </p><p>1. **Three Roller Gate**: Utilizes rotating rollers for controlled access, allowing entry only upon validation, ensuring a secure and efficient flow. </p><p>2. **Swing Gate**: Features a swinging barrier that grants passage after authentication, ideal for wide access points and accommodating individuals with mobility aids.</p><p>3. **Wing Gate**: Incorporates retractable wings providing a sleek design, suitable for high-security areas while maintaining user-friendly access for pedestrians. Each type caters to specific security and space requirements.</p></p>
